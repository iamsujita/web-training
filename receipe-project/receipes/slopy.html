Slopy Joes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup ketchup, or to taste

Directions

  1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper. Drain the liquids, then stir in the remaining ingredients.Simmer for about 30 minutes. Serve on hamburger buns.
